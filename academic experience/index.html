Academic Experience

I studied Computer Science at University of Macau for one and half year as an undergraduate student. Then I decided to study abroad and came to Canada. The first destination I went to is Dalhousie University. I quite enjoy studying there. It is a quiet and peaceful city with nice and polite people. The good atmosphere helps me focus on studies and therefore I got good academic achievement. In the winter there, I attempted to apply to transfer to University of Toronto because it is so called "the best university in Canada". In the following summer, I got the offer and decided to transfer again. Also during the summer period, I took an online course from MIT called Big data and social analytics. From then on, I know the importance of understanding a variety of skills in data and programming fields. After transferring to UofT, I added a second major Actuarial Science, which includes knowledge about economics, finance, statistics and mathematics. Then during the first summer at UofT, I took another online course from Stanford University called Machine Learning. It is a course taught by Professor Andrew Ng who is a well-known scientist in Machine and Deep Learning field. In 2018, after two-year study at UofT, I am finally going to finish my undergraduate study and take another step forward.
